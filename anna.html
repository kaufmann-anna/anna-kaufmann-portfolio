who am i?

let's see: i'm a daughter, a sister, a friend, a student, a good listener and a creator.

if i have to write a book about what would it be?

if i had to write a guide book, i would write something about "getting out of your comfortzone".

what do i love?

i love my family and my friends. i enjoy good company and music. i love to connect with people, to write songs - in a fun way - and to photograph in my free time. sometimes i like to do some running activities or just watch some great movies.

what makes me really angry?

classic answer: injustice. injustice someone is experiencing because of race, gender, sexuality or appearance. beside that, wasting food makes me sad. and when someone has a huge ego and is showing it off.

what should you know about me?

sometimes i like listening more than talking.

what had a great impact in my life?

people. my volunteer work in israel with children with heart diseases. my exchange semester in india. Both not only changed my view on life but also on myself. i got out of my comfortzone constantly, learned a lot about flexibility and connected with people around the world.

which advice would i give a previous version of myself?

a lot of things in life are a process, do not rush the waiting, good things are coming.
