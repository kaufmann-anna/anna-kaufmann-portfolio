who am i?

let's see: i am a daughter, a sister, a friend, a student, a good listener and a designer-to-be.

what did i do after my a-levels?

i went to israel to volunteer at a non-profit-organisation which helped children with heart diseases from iraq, syria and gaza to get health care and a place to live. It changed my view about things: how fast life can be over, what a privilege it is to live. I learned a lot about goodbyes.

if i had to write a book about what would it be?

if i had to write a guide book, i would write something about "getting out of your comfortzone" or "what we can learn from plants".

what do you love?

i love my family and my friends. i enjoy good company and playing music. oh and i love to read novels, to write songs and to photograph in my free time. sometimes i like to do some running activities or just watch some great movies.

what makes you really angry?

classic answer: injustice. injustice someone is experiencing because of her/his race, gender, sexuality or appearance. beside that, wasting food makes me sad. and when someone has a huge ego and is showing it off, i do not like that too.

what should you know about me?

sometimes i like listening more than talking.

which advice would i give a previous version of myself?

a lot of things in life are a process, do not rush the waiting, good things are coming.
