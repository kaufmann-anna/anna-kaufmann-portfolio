app argus

do you feel safe on your way to a destination in the dark? for the digital media design class, we, as a group of 8, decided to create an app for people, especially young women, to feel safer on their way back home or to another destination, especially in the dark.

the app has as a main feature an sos-button. this button can be used outside the app as well to call an emergency number. the user can define this number beforehand.

it is possible to call and to share the own location with certain people or with public security organizations. if the user is in a situation where she or he feels unsafe or followed, the user has the possibility to use fake-conversation audio tracks to put the follower off.

for creating the safest way back home or to a destination, there is a map. the map shows safe places where the user can go in an emergency case, lightning situation of an area and critical places. besides that, it is also possible to mark places where someone has experienced any kind of catcalling or sexual harassment.

the user can also educate herself or himself with certain articles about women's safety, cat calling etc.

we wanted to create also a possibility for people to get in contact with other people who are searching for a company on their way as well. in order to be able to get in contact, both parties have to accept the match. on the profile of someone, it is possible to see certain details about them: how many successful ways together with another person the person had, and other things the person wants to share. to avoid that some people are misuse this for abusing someone, everyone who wants to be part of this community must be verified beforehand via passport.
