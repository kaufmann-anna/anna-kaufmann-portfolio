wanderlust sculptor

this is a documentary portrait about bangalore based artist archana who is hearing impaired and wants to travel the world with her motorcycle.

tea with yukthi

this is a documentary portrait about yukthi. i met yukthi at her house in bangalore and experienced her great hospitality. we talked over a cup of tea about her life and the difficulties she is experiencing by being a transgender in the indian society.

janardhan

this is a documentary portrait about brixton cafe owner janardhan from bangalore.

the letter

this short film is about how we feel towards language. it is about the anachronism of writing a letter, of expressing words and feelings in a non-digital way. this project was a team work between sophie humbert, michelle michewicz and me for audiovisual basics class.

opener

as a common work, rosa haller and i animated this opener for the student film festival dot.mov in mannheim.

inner child

this short video is presenting sara kaufmann's fashion design project for the pforzheim university of applied sciences. the assignment was to create a fashion design outfit on the topic "protection". "protecting the inner child" was the specific topic she chose. she asked me to come up with an idea for presenting her piece. we wanted to build tension by defining two storylines and using a parallel cut. i took part in creating the concept, story and doing the editing of this short clip.
