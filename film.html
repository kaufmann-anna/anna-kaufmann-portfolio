the letter

this short film is about how we feel towards language. it is about the anachronism of writing a letter, of expressing words and feelings in a non-digital way. this project was a team work between sophie humbert, michelle michewicz and me for audiovisual basics class. here on this website it is only possible to see a compressed version of it, but i think it will still give you an idea of what we created.

opener

as a common work, rosa haller and me animated this opener for the student film festival dot.mov in mannheim.

inner child

sara kaufmann and i created this short film to present her project for the pforzheim university of applied sciences which is a fashion design outfit on the topic "protection". "protecting the inner child" was her specific issue. we wanted to build tension by defining two storylines and using a parallel cut. i took part in creating the concept, story and doing the cutting of this short film. here on this website it is only possible to see a compressed version of it, but i think it will stil give you an idea of what we created.
